- **Unified Solver**: Routes problems through precomputed tables, geometric constructions, or Hybrid Simulated Annealing (SA).

A three-stage extraction pipeline for converting natural language problems into solvable structures:

- **Stage 2: RLM-N Peeking**: Programmatic context-peeking for hypercontext analysis.
